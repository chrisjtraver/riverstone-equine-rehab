Services Offered

Riverstone Rehab offers a multi-level approach to your horse’s needs. Working with you and your referring veterinarian, we customize a comprehensive long- or short-term program based on your horse’s diagnosis.

Stage 1 Rehab: Includes stall rest, grooming, standing/specialty wraps, hand-grazing, progress reports, administering medications, management of vet, farrier, etc.

Stage 2 Rehab: Includes TheraPlate, magnetic blanket, hydrotherapy, ice boots, magnetic boots, hand-walking, monitored medical paddock turnout, and specific injury care.

Stage 3 Rehab: Includes treadmill walking, tack-walking, fitness rides, ground pen work, full training rides.

We also offer massage, chiropractic, and acupuncture therapy from our alternative medical partnerships.
